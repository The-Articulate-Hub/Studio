Protecting Personal Information

Your personal data is valuable. Keep it secure by following these tips:

  • Use different, strong passwords for each account.
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible.
  • Regularly review the permissions you've granted to apps and sites.

Example: Emma checked her old accounts and revoked access for outdated apps she no longer used.
