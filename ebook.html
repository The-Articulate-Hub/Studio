Chapter 1: Strong Passwords

Introduction: Learn the importance of strong passwords and how to create one to secure your online accounts.

  • Use at least 16 characters.
  • Include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters.
  • Don’t use personal information like your name or birthdate.

Summary: Strong passwords are the foundation of your online safety. Always use unique and complex passwords.

Chapter 2: Identifying Fake News

Introduction: Fake news can mislead and harm. Learn how to differentiate credible information from misinformation.

  • Verify the source of the information.
  • Check for supporting evidence from reputable outlets.
  • Be cautious of sensational headlines.

Summary: Always cross-check the authenticity of information to avoid falling for fake news.
