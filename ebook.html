Welcome to the eBook

In this space, we aim to support you in understanding online safety. The content is broken into chapters, each addressing key areas like recognising risks, finding help, and using the internet in a secure, confident way. Navigate using the menu on the left. Take breaks whenever you need and return at your own pace.

Chapter 1: Basics of Online Safety

This chapter covers the fundamental principles of staying safe online. We’ll start with understanding privacy settings and the importance of strong passwords, then move on to identifying trusted websites and services.

Chapter 2: Recognising Risks

Here we explore common online threats and how to spot them. From phishing emails to suspicious links, we provide tips to help you feel more confident and in control.

Chapter 3: Seeking Support

If you ever feel uncertain or upset by what you’ve seen online, it’s crucial to know where to turn. This chapter provides guidance on reaching out to trusted individuals and finding professional resources that offer help without judgement.
