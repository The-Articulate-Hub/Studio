Chapter 3: Protecting Personal Information

Introduction: Your personal information is valuable. This chapter explains how to protect it from being exploited online.

  • Limit the information you share on social media.
  • Enable two-factor authentication on your accounts.
  • Be cautious of sharing personal data on unverified websites.

Summary: Safeguarding personal information is crucial to maintaining privacy and security online.

Chapter 4: Cyberbullying and Online Etiquette

Introduction: Understanding cyberbullying and maintaining proper online etiquette are essential to a positive digital experience.

  • Report and block users who engage in cyberbullying.
  • Be respectful and empathetic when communicating online.
  • Familiarize yourself with platform guidelines and policies.

Summary: By addressing cyberbullying and practising good etiquette, you contribute to a safer online community.

Chapter 5: Privacy Settings on Social Media

Introduction: Social media platforms collect a lot of personal data. This chapter teaches how to adjust privacy settings to protect your information.

  • Set your profile to private.
  • Limit who can see your posts and personal details.
  • Turn off location sharing.

Summary: Customising privacy settings helps you stay in control of your online presence.

Chapter 6: Recognising and Avoiding Phishing Scams

Introduction: Phishing scams trick users into revealing sensitive information. Learn how to spot and avoid them.

  • Check email addresses carefully for typos.
  • Avoid clicking on suspicious links.
  • Never share personal information through email or messages.

Summary: Being vigilant against phishing scams can save you from identity theft and financial loss.

Chapter 7: Online Shopping Safety

Introduction: Online shopping is convenient but comes with risks. Learn how to shop safely online.

  • Only shop on trusted websites with HTTPS encryption.
  • Use secure payment methods like credit cards or PayPal.
  • Avoid public Wi-Fi when making transactions.

Summary: Following these steps ensures your personal and financial information stays safe while shopping online.
